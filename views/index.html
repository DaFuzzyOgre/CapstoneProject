Welcome to IU Kokomo Library Study Room Scheduler

Reserve a Study Room Edit Existing Study Room Cancel Existing Study Room

  • Reservations may be made by currently enrolled IU Kokomo students up to one week in advance, but no less than 24 hours in advance.
  • Room reservations must be made using your primary IU email address exactly as listed in the IU Directory. Reservations from other or non-IU email addresses will not be approved.
  • Study room users without reservations must vacate the room when an individual or group with a reservation arrives. A daily schedule of study room reservations will be posted outside each room.
  • No-shows: If the room is not claimed within 15 minutes of the reserved time, the reservation will be cancelled and the study room will be available to others for the remainder of the time reserved.
  • The Library is not responsible for unattended belongings. Materials left unattended in a study room for more than 10 minutes will be removed and taken to the Circulation/Reserves Service Desk.
  • Report any problems with the rooms to the Departmental Secretary, Charla Calvert: KA 233, ccalver@iu.edu, 765-455-9511.

All study rooms are available for use from the opening of the Library until ten (10) minutes before closing Study rooms are available for IU Kokomo Students only. Reservations accepted Monday – Friday.

The study rooms below may be reserved for half-hour increments, up to three consecutive hours per day, three times per week (Monday – Friday), per individual or group. When not reserved, and all day on Saturday and Sunday, all study rooms are available on a first-come, first-served basis.
